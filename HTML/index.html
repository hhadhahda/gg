The Legend Lives On

The Nissan Skyline is a line of compact cars, sports cars and compact executive cars originally produced by the Prince Motor Company starting in 1957, and then by Nissan after the two companies merged in 1967. The Skyline GT-R is the most iconic and sought-after model in the Skyline range, and the R34 generation is considered by many to be the ultimate GT-R.
